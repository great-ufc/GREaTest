GREaTest Card Game

A card game for teaching software testing

More

The Game

The GreaTest Card Game is a educational hybrid card game created for teaching test software, to make the learning experience more attractive

In the proposed game, the challenges are scenarios of use of an application and players have to match a type of test that when performed may reveal the situation described.

The game can be played by up to 6 players!

Objective

The game aims to stimulate the learning of concepts software testing and introduce the testing area to students at the beginning of the course through a playful experience, group, natural and collaborative, promoting interaction between students and improving motivation.

The types of software testing approached are:

  • Acceptance Testing
  • Performance Testing
  • Stress Testing
  • Functional Testing
  • Security Testing
  • Usability Testing

How to play:

  • Step 1: place 5 cards from Deck of challenges (red cards) upon the table, with their face up descriptions. Each player gets 5 cards from the Deck of Game (green cards)
  • Step 2: During it's turn, each player may get one more cards from the Deck of Game (green cards). In case of owning a Tester card in table, the player may execute up to 2 tests. To execute the test, the player choses a Testing card from it's hand and joins with the challenge card in table.
  • Step 3: if the answer is contained in the challenge card's back, the player throws the dice and if the number is present inside the interval of the right answer, wins a point and gets a card from the Deck of Bônus (blue cards), with effects that may help the player (e.g., Spy, who permmits stole a random card from other player's hand).
  • For each challenge card that is answered (right or wrong), another one must replace it.
  • Win who has 6 points or has the most points at the end of the cards of Challenges.

The GreaTest Helper

To auxilliary the gameplay, we created an Android App : The GreaTest Helper.

Our app was built with the purpose of improve some practical aspects of the gameplay, e.g., to manage the player's points and the time for every round. Beyond, it's necessary to have a dice. By this idea, the conception of the app arrived spontaneously.

The GreaTest Helper can be obtained for free!

Features:

  • Personalized points counter
  • Digital dice
  • A Timer
  • Contact with us
  • The rules of the game, well explained
